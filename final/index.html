About Us

Students who identify as Azerbaijani or are interested in Azerbaijani culture are welcome in our organization.

Founded in 2022, Azerbaijanis at Berkeley is a student run non-profit organization with a goal to create a professional, academic and social environment for any individuals interested in the culture of Azerbaijan.
